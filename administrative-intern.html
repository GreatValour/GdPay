. Overview

Are you a numbers person with an eye for detail? Do you think numbers are fun? Do you enjoy analyzing data and Financial reports? Are you interested in gaining valuable experience in the field of Merchant Financial Services like Accounting, Budget,POS Payment systems and Purchasing? If so, GD Pay Solution has the perfect position for you! GD Pay Solution invites you to apply to join the team as an Administrative Intern. As the Administrative Intern also referred to as the Finance Intern, your primary duties will be to assist in and complete a wide variety of administrative, technical, and relevant assignments in support of the Merchant Services Agency.

Responsibilities

  • Will learn accounts payable and accounts receivable.
  • Handle Domestic and international invoices.
  • Participate in projects including but not limited to: Cost savings, Payment Gateways, POS systems and many more.
  • Resolve any payment-related issues or discrepancies
  • Assist with payment gateways and systems, POS systems, and Cash applications.

This job description is not designed to cover or contain a comprehensive listing of activities, duties or responsibilities that are required of the employee for this job. Duties, responsibilities, and activities may change at any time with or without notice.

Qualifications

Education/credentials
  • Must be currently enrolled in an accredited university or college.

Job-related experience

  • Customarily has at least one or more years of collections experience

Job-Type

  • Remote

Job-related skills/competencies

  • Excellent problem solving and analytical skills.
  • Excellent analytical and problem-solving skills
  • Proficient in Ms-Excel, PowerPoint and Word.
  • Effective communication and interpersonal skills
  • Effective time management skill

Benefits

Pay offered to a successful candidate will be based on several factors including the candidate's education, Previous work experience, specific job duties, certifications, etc.

  • 401(k) Retirement Plan
  • Medical, Vision, Prescription, Telehealth, & Dental Plans
  • Life & Disability Insurance
  • Paid Time Off & Extended Illness Days Offered
  • Commuter Benefits
  • Employee Discounts

This job requires access to confidential and critical information, requiring ongoing discretion and secure information management.

We will ensure that individuals with disabilities are provided reasonable accommodation to participate in the job application or interview process, to perform essential job functions, and to receive other benefits and privileges of employment. Please contact us to request accommodation.
