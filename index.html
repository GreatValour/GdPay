Interchange plus pricing ensures that merchants alway pay the least amount of fees no matter of the card type. Fees are collected at cost on interchange (amount that is paid to card issuing bank) and payment brands (Visa®, MasterCard®, Discover® and American Express®) The merchant service provider will have a small markup fee for servicing the account.
