Our philosophy is simple; we've asked merchants and resellers what they wanted over the years. We specialize in working with all sized businesses. We offer solutions from the traditional point-of-sale transaction processing, electronic check conversion, gift and loyalty card processing to a proprietary ATM debit payroll / international funds transfer card. Additionally we can help with an array of ACH electronic debit services including file transmissions and a sophisticated WEB-based database platform for merchants to privately perform their own recurring debit activities.  In other words, GDpay provides you the affordable information services you need to compete with the retail elite. Craig Yox Managing Director

Global Data Payments,LLC (GDPay) is pleased to introduce Craig Yox as a Managing Director.
 
His background has been in computer service sales and support for Bell Atlantic, Control Data and Sun Microsystems.
From 1992 to 2007 he was responsible for operating the Western Area Service Sales Business Unit at Sun Microsystems, a Fortune 100 Company, responsible for generating revenues of over $220 million a year.
Craig started working in the credit card processing service business with his son Kevin, in 2007.
As a managing partner at GDPay his oversight of training and support for new agents, superior customer service and executive leadership has been invaluable.


Kevin Yox Managing Director

After twelve years in the merchant service industry as an agent, public speaker, author and website developer, I decided to incorporate my own company. When I started GDpay I wanted to operate a self funded debt free company having complete control to provide merchant services to my clients with a special emphasis on education, service and attention to details.
 
Our clients continue to applaud our commitment to ongoing support and service. We know that the highest form of compliment is when our clients reward us with continual referrals.
 
As the company grew, I invited my Father to join me as a managing partner to offer support and training to our growing network of agents.
 
The imprimis factor of our company is to develop lifetime relationships with our clients, agents and employees while maintaining integrity in the process.
