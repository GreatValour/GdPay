The Process

You focus on your core business and let us find the best payment solution for your customer. Our knowledgeable agents will work with your customer to understand their needs and will tailor a solution for them implementing it quickly.

-Your customer is contacted by one of our account managers.
-The merchant completes the online application.
-Underwriting reviews the application.
-Upon approval, gateway or equipment is deployed
-The merchant calls the 24/7 customer service team to finalize the set-up.
-Our customer service team is available anytime your client may have a question.
